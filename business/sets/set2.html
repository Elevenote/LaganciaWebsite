The Version 0.1.1 Prototype is the second prototype version of the Buddy set. The Buddy set is a Futuristic AR
Headset. The Interface consists of basic applications such as Youtube, Google Chrome, Camera, "Chip", which handles
the information regarding the chip that is installed on the Buddy set, and an additional unique feature: Hangout!.
The application: Hangout! shows your "Buddy" on your screen and you can even talk to it through voice recognition! It
talks according to the personality embedded on the installed chip and can even talk, this is only compatible with Chip
versions 0.1.2 and above. This version is perfect for those who want to experience the joy of having an AR
companion, being able to talk to it, and can even remember, through the chip without breaking the bank.
