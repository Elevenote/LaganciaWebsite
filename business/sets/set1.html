The Version 0.1.0 Prototype is the first prototype version of the Buddy set. The Buddy set is a Futuristic AR Headset that tracks
the eye movement of the user to interact with the interface. The Interface consists of basic applications such as
Youtube, Google Chrome, Camera, and "Chip", which handles the information regarding the chip that is installed on the
Buddy set. This version is perfect for those who want to experience the joy of having an AI voice companion through the
chip without breaking the bank.
